Alalaam

العَلّام — سِيَرٌ في سياقها

Lives, in context. Alalaam places one historical figure at the centre of their world and draws that world as an evidence graph: who they met, who they may have met, and who they only knew through books.

01

One life, drawn as evidence

حياة واحدة، مرسومة بالدليل

The subject sits at the centre; everyone else takes their distance by certainty. An inner ring for documented colleagues, a dashed ring for plausible contemporaries, a dotted ring for those known only through books — the sources he inherited on one side, the heirs of his method on the other.

How to read the lines

Met & worked beside him documented
May have crossed paths shared his city & decades
Knew them through books a source he read — arrow in
Knew him through books an heir who read him — arrow out

What’s coming

The Majlis Explorer مجلس

An interactive radial map of one figure’s world — rings of certainty, a sliding profile panel, multi-focal comparisons — fully bilingual English / Arabic with true right-to-left reading.

EXPLORER

al-Khwarizmi & his world الخوارزمي وعالمه

The demo subject: the House of Wisdom, the caliphs who commissioned it, the sources he inherited and the heirs of his method. An initial roster of 23 figures — an example set, not a limit; the roster keeps growing.

ROSTER

An editorial pipeline خط تحرير

From a Google Sheet, through a validating command-line engine, to a living site — every claim checked against the model before it reaches the map, and the published site refreshing as the record grows.
